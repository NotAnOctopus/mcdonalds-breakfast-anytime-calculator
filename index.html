The McDonald's Breakfast Anytime Calculator

So let's suppose hypothetically that you find yourself wandering through the bustling streets of Sao Paulo one day at 6 in the evening, when all of a sudden, a flash of inspiration enlightens you as to the one thing in this world that you are missing in your life: a McDonald's sausage and egg McMuffin. You immediately make it your mission to get a hold of one as soon as possible. But as everybody knows, McDonald's only open their breakfast menu between 6 and 10:30 in the morning! You cry in despair, because you can't wait that long! How, you wonder, do I track down a McDonald's serving breakfast as soon as possible, thus reaching my path to enlightenment in life?

If this scenario has ever occurred to you, this online calculator is here to help you fulfill your destiny. Simply enter your location and time, and it'll tell you where to fly to in order to get a McDonald's breakfast as soon as possible, assuming that they only open worldwide between 6 and 10:30am. In this case, your answer will be to get to Cape Town in South Africa, taking you 7 hours and 54 minutes, compared to the 12 hours it would have taken you if you'd stayed in Sao Paulo.
